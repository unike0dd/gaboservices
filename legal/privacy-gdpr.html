Privacy and GDPR

Effective date: March 18, 2026

Gabriel Services is committed to handling personal data lawfully, transparently, and securely. This notice explains what personal data we may collect through this website, why we use it, how long we keep it, and what rights individuals may have under applicable privacy laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) where it applies.

1. Categories of personal data

  • Identity and contact details, such as name, email address, phone number, company name, and role.
  • Inquiry and correspondence information submitted through forms, email, chatbot, or other contact channels.
  • Recruitment information, such as CVs, cover letters, work history, and related application materials.
  • Technical and usage information, such as IP address, browser type, device characteristics, security logs, and website interaction data.

2. Purposes of processing

  • Responding to inquiries, requests for proposals, support needs, or commercial conversations.
  • Evaluating job applications and managing recruitment processes.
  • Operating, securing, and improving the website and its forms, chatbot, and verification mechanisms.
  • Complying with legal, regulatory, contractual, and record-keeping obligations.
  • Protecting against fraud, abuse, unauthorized access, and other security threats.

3. Lawful bases under GDPR

  • Consent: where you choose to submit certain information or accept optional cookies.
  • Pre-contractual steps: where processing is necessary to discuss services or prepare a proposal at your request.
  • Legitimate interests: for website administration, information security, service improvement, and business communications, provided such interests are not overridden by your rights.
  • Legal obligation: where retention or disclosure is required by applicable law or regulatory requirements.

4. Data sharing

Personal data may be shared on a need-to-know basis with hosting providers, cloud infrastructure providers, security and anti-abuse services, analytics providers where enabled, recruitment support tools, and professional advisers. We may also disclose data where required to comply with law, enforce rights, or protect the website, our users, or the public.

5. International transfers

If personal data is transferred outside the country or region where it was collected, Gabriel Services will seek to use appropriate safeguards where required by law, such as contractual protections or reliance on recognized adequacy mechanisms.

6. Retention

  • Inquiry and commercial correspondence is retained only for as long as needed to respond, follow up, and maintain reasonable business records.
  • Recruitment records are retained for the duration of the recruitment process and a limited period afterward, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.
  • Security, audit, and technical logs may be retained for operational resilience, fraud prevention, and legal compliance.

7. Data subject rights

Subject to applicable law, you may have the right to request access to your personal data, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, or objection to certain processing. Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw that consent at any time for future processing. You may also have the right to lodge a complaint with a competent supervisory authority.

8. Security measures

Gabriel Services applies reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to reduce the risk of loss, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration of personal data. No internet transmission or storage environment can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

9. Cookies and website technologies

For information about cookies, local storage, and optional consent preferences, please review our Cookies Consent page.

10. Contact and requests

To make a privacy request or ask a question about this notice, please use the contact details provided on our Contact page and clearly identify your request so it can be handled efficiently.

11. Changes to this notice

We may update this privacy notice from time to time to reflect changes in our processing activities, legal requirements, or operational practices. The latest version and effective date will remain published here.
