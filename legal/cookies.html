Cookies Consent

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. They help websites function, remember preferences, and (if enabled) measure usage and improve user experience.

We may also use similar technologies (local storage, pixels, SDKs) that work like cookies.

2. How we use cookies

Gabriel Services may use cookies for the purposes below:

  • Strictly Necessary: required for core site functionality and security.
  • Preferences: remember choices (language, UI settings).
  • Analytics: understand usage to improve the Services (only if you consent where required).
  • Marketing: measure campaigns or personalize offers (only if you consent where required).
