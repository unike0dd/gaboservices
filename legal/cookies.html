This page explains how Gabriel Services uses cookies and similar technologies on this website, and how you can manage your consent preferences.

1. What cookies are

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function, remember preferences, measure performance, and support security features. Similar technologies such as local storage, pixels, and scripts may be used for related purposes.

4. Browser controls

Most browsers let you block or delete cookies through their settings. If you disable strictly necessary cookies, parts of the website may stop working correctly. Device-level or browser-level settings may override preferences saved on this page.

5. Third-party technologies

Some website features may rely on third-party providers such as security verification, analytics, embedded media, or external scheduling/contact tools. When those providers place cookies or similar identifiers, their own privacy notices may also apply.
