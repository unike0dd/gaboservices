Gabriel Services

Gabriel Services was built with operational success and business sustainability at its core. We believe long-term success comes from small, sustainable details.

When opportunities are left unresolved

That is where we step in.

We make sure no opportunity slips through the cracks. Every detail is handled so your business stays organized, responsive, and running smoothly.

We step in by handling the details and bringing structure to fragmented operations. We integrate into your workflows, align with your processes, and focus on execution so your team can stay focused on growth.

Our approach is simple:

  • We improve productivity.

  • We deliver high-quality execution.
  • We operate with practical and cost-conscious service delivery.

What we support

We focus on four operational areas:

  • Logistics
  • Customer Relations
  • Administrative Back Office
  • IT Support (Level I and Level II)

What we handle

Logistics Operations

We help keep your logistics organized, visible, and moving:

  • Dispatch coordination
  • Shipment tracking (ocean, air, and ground)
  • Customer communication and follow-ups
  • Invoicing and billing support
  • Documentation coordination

Customer Relations

We help maintain customer satisfaction and resolution flow:

  • Ticket follow-up and resolution tracking
  • Customer satisfaction follow-ups (CSAT support)
  • Billing clarification support
  • First contact resolution support
  • Customer communication management

Administrative Back Office

We support leadership and daily business operations:

  • Executive administrative support
  • Calendar and scheduling coordination
  • Email and communication management
  • Document organization and preparation
  • Vendor coordination
  • Internal operational support
  • Travel planning and coordination

IT Support (Level I & Level II)

We provide structured technical support coverage:

Level I Support

  • Help desk intake and ticket creation
  • Basic troubleshooting
  • End-user support
  • Account access assistance
  • Escalation coordination

Level II Support

  • Advanced troubleshooting
  • Incident investigation
  • System and workflow support
  • Root cause analysis
  • Resolution ownership after escalation
